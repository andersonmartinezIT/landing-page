What is Nomad Spot?

The best mobile APP for digital nomads

Nomad Spot was founded by Two brothers, Zack and Wayne Osbourne from New Haven, Connecticut in 2018. They would travel to south east Asia to escape to cold weathers of North East America.

During their travels they met other nomads and learned about the many issues that other apps and websites were not solving for digital nomads. Zack and Wayne encounter issues during trips that hinder their life as nomads

The brothers put their heads together to solve similar problems that Digital Nomads around the world were facing and thus Nomad Spot was born.

What do you get with the Nomad Spot APP?

Country Guide

Nomad Spot analyzes the best countries for the digital nomad's needs. Lifestyle, activities, socializing and more

City Guide

The Nomad Spot has an indept information about the top cities around the world and how to navigate them with taxis, trains, buses and affordable options

Hotel Guide

Our databse will give you the best hotels, hostels, apartments and room rentals around the world suited for digital nomads.

WIFI Guide

Best guide on getting WIFI Devices and phone at airports around the world as well as best WIFI spots at any desired country

Remote Jobs

Nomads will have access to job posting for remote jobs that are currently hiring. We do our best to filter out scam or fake job postings.

Meet Up Guide

Nomads will have access to meet up groups for digital nomads around the world and other related events

Flight Guide

Members will have access to the database of flights. We keep track of the lowest prices ranging from $1,000 or lower

News & Reources

Members will have access to news suited for digital nomads, foreign visas plans, investments, best travel insurance, podcast, travel tips, travel tools and much more
